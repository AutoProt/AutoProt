MS2 precursor isolation width TICThe median MS TIC in the isolation window for all MS2 scans.

MS2 scans with precursor as BP percentageHow large a fraction of all MS2 scans that have a base peak within the precursor isolation window. If high, this could indicate a problem with poor fragmentation or lots of interference?
MS2 scans XIC above precursor percentageHow large a fraction of MS2 TIC is above the isolation window. A high value is desirable, i.e. this indicates good fragmentation unless of course only charge reduction takes place.
